OCD Self-Check Quiz

1. Do you find it hard to stop unwanted thoughts?

2. Do you wash your hands many times, even when they’re not dirty?

3. Do you check things like doors or locks again and again?

4. Do you feel upset if things aren’t in perfect order?

5. Do you count things like steps or items often?

6. Do you get scared something bad will happen if you don’t do certain things?

7. Do you avoid things because they might be “dirty”?

8. Do you repeat words or actions to feel okay?

9. Do you feel tired from always trying to stop or fix your thoughts?

10. Do you hide your habits because of fear of judgment?

Note: This form is for educational purposes only. For professional help, consult a licensed mental health expert.
